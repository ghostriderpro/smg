ABOUT US

At Synergies Manufacturing Group, we stand as an embodiment of excellence, driven by our affiliation with Synergies Sourcing Bangladesh Limited, a global frontrunner in apparel sourcing and supply chain management. Our roots in the industry are deep, grounded in a legacy of innovation and quality. As a subsidiary of Synergies Sourcing Bangladesh Limited, we inherit a dedication to impeccable craftsmanship and forward-thinking practices.

We redefine the boundaries of apparel manufacturing by fusing the artistry of design with the precision of technology. With state-of-the-art facilities and an exceptional team, we fashion clothing that transcends expectations. Sustainability is not just a goal but a cornerstone of our ethos. We are committed to weaving eco-conscious practices into every thread of our production process.

Partnering with us means collaborating with a company that values ethics and integrity. With Synergies Manufacturing Group, you gain more than a manufacturer; you gain a partner invested in your success. Join us as we weave the future of apparel manufacturing, one stitch of innovation at a time.

THE FOUNDATION OF ANY COLLECTION

In SMG, we offer research and development, design, production, and operations support.

Research & Development

In Synergies Worldwide, we believe that Research & Development is the foundation of any collection. Our R&D teams in different locations source and develop the latest fabrics, finishes and washes ahead of the market. The team works closely with our extensive network of mills in order to provide customers with quality products in their desired price.

Design

Our team of designers travel around the world to look for design inspirations and novel ideas that breathe new designs to our customers specification. We add value to our customers’ inhouse design teams and enhance their product while keeping true to their brand identity.

Production

Our presence in Asia enables us to manage our manufacturing operations effectively. We have experienced technical experts on-site who monitor and control the quality at our factories. With our experienced and skilled on-site Quality Control teams, we can provide our partners with the transparency and controls needed to measure the quality of each order.

Operations Support

In Synergies Worldwide, these teams collaborate with their counterparts to ensure that operations are carried out smoothly and with efficiency across the supply chain.
